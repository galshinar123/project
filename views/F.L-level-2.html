one leg Front-lever

The single leg Front Lever is a static Calisthenics element. It is a great exercise in order to learn the Front Lever, as the athlete needs to perform smaller force in order to counter the legs weight.
prefom 5 sets of maximum hold with 1 minute between sets.
preform 5 sets of negative Tuck-lever for as much time as you can
goal - hold the tuck lever for 15 seconds
